Privacy Policy for holos

Last updated: December 14, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to holos ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of any information you provide while using our sticker creation application.

Information We Collect

holos is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Photos: Photos you select for sticker creation are processed locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.
  • Usage Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app experience. This data does not personally identify you.
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash reports may be collected to help us fix bugs and improve app stability.

How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used solely to:

  • Provide and improve the sticker creation functionality
  • Fix bugs and improve app performance
  • Understand how users interact with the app to make it better

Data Storage and Security

All sticker processing happens locally on your device. Your photos and created stickers are stored in your device's local storage and are not transmitted to external servers. We implement appropriate security measures to protect any data that may be collected.

Third-Party Services

holos may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics: To understand app usage patterns (anonymous data only)
  • In-App Purchases: Handled through Apple's App Store, subject to Apple's privacy policy

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we may have about you
  • Request deletion of any data
  • Opt-out of analytics collection through your device settings

Children's Privacy

holos does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy
